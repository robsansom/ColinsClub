PRD

Project: Colin Cue Club (Demo Website)

Purpose

Create a playful demo website for a fictional pool club to demonstrate what a real website + future app could look like for a pool venue business.

This is not a production product.
Goal = fast, visually impressive, slightly comedic, feature-showcase.

Primary audience: Colin (future venue owner)

Success metric:
He immediately understands the potential and says something like “oh wow we could actually do this”.

⸻

Product Vision

A premium-looking members pool club website inspired by Maxi Lounge.

Tone progression:
	1.	Looks professional and believable at first
	2.	Gradually reveals humour and easter eggs
	3.	Demonstrates real future product features (booking + app)

⸻

Core User Story

“As a potential club member, I want to see what the venue offers and be able to book tables, join membership, and understand the future app.”

⸻

Site Type

Marketing site + fake booking UI (front-end only)

No backend required.

All interactions can be simulated.

⸻
